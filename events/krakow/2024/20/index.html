Emancipation: Why the heck would a tech writer use enterprise tools?

We are looking at a scenario which has the potential to replace a huge CCMS solution from a serious vendor with community-supported open-source tools. Do the costs of maintenance swell up to justify the vendor fee? And what situations are better for open source vs. enterprise? Is it time for a revolution? Come and find out.
